The Problem

Learning to code should not be
expensive, scary, or confusing.

Most beginners give up before they start — not because coding is hard, but because affordable, personal support simply does not exist at an accessible price point.

Too Expensive

Traditional tutoring platforms charge $60 to $120 per hour. For most families, regular sessions simply are not realistic.

Too Intimidating

Online tutorials and video courses overwhelm beginners with jargon and zero personal feedback, leading to high dropout rates.

No Foundation Built

Schools rarely offer hands-on programming before Grade 11. Students fall behind before they have even had a chance to begin.

The Solution

CodeCoach fills the gap.
Accessible. Personal. Effective.

A home-based, online tutoring service that pairs beginner students with relatable peer tutors for personalized JavaScript sessions — at a price designed to be within reach for every student.

Process

From booking to coding
in four simple steps.

1

Book a Session Online

Visit the CodeCoach website and choose a time slot through the Calendly booking system. A quick intake form captures experience level and learning goals.

2

Your Tutor Prepares a Custom Lesson

Based on the intake form, your assigned tutor builds a personalized session plan tailored to exactly where you are and where you want to go.

3

Join Your Live 1-on-1 Zoom Session

One hour of screen-sharing, live coding practice, and real-time explanation — no pre-recorded content, just you and your coach working through it together.

4

Receive Your Recap and Rebook

After every session you receive an email summary of what was covered along with practice challenges. Lock in your next weekly slot to keep building momentum.
